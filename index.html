About Me

I grew up in Coimbatore — a city built on textile mills, where raw thread becomes cloth through patient, structured work. That's basically how I approach code: pull the thread until you understand exactly how it's woven in, then build on top of it deliberately.

I'm largely self-taught. Most of what I know came from pulling apart tutorials, tracing through unfamiliar code line by line, and asking "why does this actually work" instead of just copying it. That habit followed me from my B.Sc in Information Technology straight into my first real job.

"I like the unglamorous parts of software — turning messy, unstructured data into something reliable, wiring up authentication correctly, making sure a deployment doesn't fall over at 2 a.m."

I joined AltiusNXT Technologies as an intern in August 2025. Within six months I was converted to a full-time Junior Software Developer — building AI-integrated internal tools the company actually runs on, not practice projects. Outside of assigned work, I keep learning in public: tutorials, side experiments, rebuilding things until I fully understand them.
